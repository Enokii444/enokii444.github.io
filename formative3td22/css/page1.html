About Me

Hi! I'm Hailie Felicia Alberto, also known as Zakii — a 19-year-old Multimedia Arts student from FEU Institute of Technology, currently in my 2nd year.

Art has always been my way of making sense of the world. Every piece I create feels like a reflection of a different side of me — my thoughts, my emotions, and the stories I want to tell. Creating something with my own hands, whether digital or traditional, gives me a sense of fulfillment that nothing else can replace.

My Craft

My specialization is in Digital Illustration and Graphic Design, but I see myself as an all-around artist. I love exploring different mediums that allow me to express creativity in unique ways — from digital work to traditional forms like pottery, painting, and henna tattoos.

I offer creative services in:

Digital Illustration

Character art, concept designs, and digital paintings that bring imagination to life.

Branding & Visual Identity

Creating cohesive brand experiences through logos, color palettes, and visual systems.

Graphic Design for Marketing

Eye-catching promotional materials, social media graphics, and marketing collateral.

Traditional Art Forms

Pottery, painting, and henna tattoos — hands-on creativity with tangible results.

Each craft I do represents a part of who I am — the colors I feel, the stories I imagine, and the satisfaction of bringing art to life with my own hands.

Current Role

I'm currently a Marketing Committee Officer for PRISM, the Pioneers of Relentless and Innovative Storytellers in Multimedia Arts.

Through this role, I've strengthened my skills in visual communication, branding, and promotional design, and learned how to connect ideas with creativity.
