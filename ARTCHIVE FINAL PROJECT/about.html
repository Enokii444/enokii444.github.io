About Us

The people behind the local catalog

Hey there!

As aspiring art students, we believe that local art deserves to be seen, remembered, and respected. Many traditional and unique Filipino art forms are slowly disappearing, not because they lack beauty, but because they lack recognition. Through our art catalog, we aim to bring these pieces back into the spotlight and honor the artists who keep our culture alive. Artchives Lokal is our way of preserving what is fading, and reminding people that these artworks are a part of our identity worth celebrating and protecting.Ëœ
